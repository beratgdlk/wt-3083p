The Devil In Opera

"The Devil in Opera" is an unmissable example of gothic art. It invites the audience to the majestic roof of the opera. This work was created using artificial intelligence technology and proves the creative potential of the digital age. The painting features a demonic figure who cries on the roof and listens to music with headphones, creating a dramatic and impressive scene. "The Devil in Opera" is a masterpiece created with artificial intelligence that offers viewers a visual feast and deep thoughts. This work reflects the creative potential of the future at the point where technology meets art.

Berat Güdelek
July 20, 2022

Starry Night With AI

This AI-generated redesign reinterprets the original work with a different artistic language while maintaining the characteristic starry night landscape. The circular brushstrokes and intense use of colour characteristic of Van Gogh's style are faithfully reproduced by AI, offering a fresh visual experience.

Berat Güdelek

NEW
The Evolution

This artefact boldly reinterprets the process of human evolution, presenting a compelling visual account of our journey from monkey to man. Created by artificial intelligence, the work includes space images and a striking symbolic depiction of evolution. This visual journey from the beginning of evolution to the present day powerfully emphasises the complexity and continuity of evolutionary change from a scientific and artistic point of view. The painting stands out as a thought-provoking work of art that challenges the place and history of humanity at the point where nature and technology converge.

Berat Güdelek
March 3, 2023
